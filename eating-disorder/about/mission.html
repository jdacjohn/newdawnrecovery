Our Mission

Mission

Our mission is to provide our clients with the tools for positive and lasting change in a safe and compassionate setting where physical, spiritual, and emotional healing can be cultivated. Measurable outcomes are utilized to ensure that a high level of consumer satisfaction, cost efficiency, and improvement of services are achieved at every stage of recovery.

Purposes

New Dawn provides treatment and support services for families and individuals affected by the eating disorder of a loved one. In support of our mission New Dawn’s purposes are:

  1. To serve and support individuals and families recovering from the effects of eating disorders.
  2. To educate the community regarding the nature of eating disorders.
  3. To promote the basic human rights, safety, health and dignity as well as the independence and self-sufficiency of the persons served.
  4. To continuously work towards providing an environment that is responsive to the need and expectations of persons served.

Values

New Dawn believes in the following core values:

  • Everyone should have the opportunity to be free from his or her eating disorder.
  • Everyone has the right to seek treatment and commit to recovery on their own terms.
  • Everyone has the right to be treated with compassion.
  • Everyone shall be treated with dignity and respect.
