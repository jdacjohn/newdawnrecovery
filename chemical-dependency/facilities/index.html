Locate an Adult or Teen Drug Rehab Program among the New Dawn Recovery Facilities

 

Whether you’re looking for an outpatient or inpatient drug treatment program, New Dawn Recovery offers the facilities and support to help individuals break free from their chemical dependencies. Providing both adult and adolescent drug rehab, New Dawn understands that life experiences can play a major role in accelerating or triggering chemical dependency. New Dawn’s Detox, Residential and Outpatient rehabilitation programs are “client centered” and therefore specifically tailored to meet each individual’s needs.  Our clinical staff takes a holistic approach that addresses each person’s unique physical, emotional, psychological, and spiritual struggles so that the underlying influences on the disease can be identified and addressed.

 

To find out more information about New Dawn’s teen and adult drug treatment facilities, please call our toll free number (866) 969-4300. One of our experienced staff members will speak with you about the services and programs offered at each rehabilitation facility. You can also fill out the online form on the Contact Us page with your questions. Your call or email will be completely confidential.
